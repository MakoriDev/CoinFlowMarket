When does owallet.com process withdrawal requests?
Instant withdrawal may take 5 to 30 min to process. However, please note that in the event of high network congestion, withdrawals may take longer time.
Is withdrawal fee already included in the withdrawal amount I have indicated?
No, the withdrawal fee will charged beyond the indicated withdrawal amount. If traders want to withdraw all the withdrawable amount, traders can click on the "All" button and system will automatically input the amount after fees.
Does owallet.com support withdrawals to a Smart Contract ETH wallet address?
No, owallet.com does not support ETH withdrawals via Smart Contracts method. Instead, owallet.com only supports ETH withdrawals via Direct Transfer method.
Does owallet.com support withdrawals to a segwit BTC wallet address?
Yes, owallet.com supports withdrawals to segwit BTC wallet addresses starting with "bc1", also known as bech32 addresses.
Can traders fix their own withdrawal fees on owallet.com?
At the moment, no. However, owallet.com is considering enabling this option for traders to determine their own withdrawal fees in the future.
